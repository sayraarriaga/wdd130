Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of my white water rafting website is to build revenue. To do this I want to give the customers the opportunity to know what we offer as a vacation company. I would like to help potential clients which want to try rifting for the first time to know what to expect and to find answers to their doubts, to know our safety measures and what kind of activities can they live in white water rifting. And for those with experience in rifting, I would like to show why our company has what they are looking for to have a safe and adventure time. This site should show all the activities that our company offers retreats, vacations, scouting activities, group trips, and solo adventures.

Audience

The audience from my site ranges from ages 13 to 100. For those who are looking for adventure or relaxation like athletes, families, groups of friends, couples, and all of those that want to disconnect for a while and have an exciting experience. My target audience usually accesses my site on laptops and phones.
