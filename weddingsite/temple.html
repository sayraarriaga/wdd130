Temple Sealing

We will celebrate our Temple sealing on September 29 2023 in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints Temple in Monterrey Nuevo Leon, (which is 4 hours away from our city) at 1:00 pm

To attend the Temple sealing you will need to:

-Be a Church Member with a Temple Endowment

- Be in the Temple 30 minutes before

- Dress in formal and modest clothes:

Men: white Shirt and tie

Women: Dress or Skirt

If you are not a member of the church or you do nott have a Temple Endowment, you are welcome to be in the Temple Gardens or the chapel next to the Temple with some of our family members, and join us at the photo shoot after the sealing
